Death by Global Warming

Due to all of the pollution created in the last century, Earth's Global Warming is currently in a critical state. Global warming has sped up by 10% and our situation is rapidly becoming worse. If global warming is not significantly reduced by the year 2100, Earth's high temperatures wil result in raised sea levels, a substantial decrease in habitable lands, less drinking water, many more human deaths, and mass food shortages.

Death by the Sun

When the Sun begins to die and turn into a red gaint, it will lose stability and start ballooning in size. While this is happening, its temperature will also continue to increase. In about about 2 billion years, at the latest, the oceans will be boiling and the earth will no longer be hospitable to life. In about 7.5 billion years the Sun will have completely evaporated the earth.
